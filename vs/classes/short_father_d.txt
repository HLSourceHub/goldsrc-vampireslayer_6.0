CLASS: Slayer

WEAPONS:

STAKE AND CRUCIFIX 
PUMP ACTION SHOTGUN
DOUBLE BARREL SHOTGUN

HOW TO PLAY:

Knock Vampires down using a shotgun then stake the
body to kill them completely. Use the secondary attack
with STAKE selected to become immune to Vampire 
attacks while Father D is speaking. To change team
press ENTER whilst dead.

QUOTE: "I kick ass for The Lord!"
