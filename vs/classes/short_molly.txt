CLASS: Slayer

WEAPONS:	

STAKE AND COLT GOVERNMENT PISTOL
MICRO-UZI
CROSSBOW

HOW TO PLAY:

Knock the Vampires down then stake them using the
primary attack with the STAKE AND COLT weapon
selected. Alternatively, kill Vampires using the
stakes fired from the CROSSBOW. Aim for the heart!
To change team press ENTER whilst dead.

QUOTE: "Vampire slaying is my life, 
	and theirs hopefully!"
