CLASS: Slayer

WEAPONS:

POOL CUE
THUNDER5
WINCHESTER RIFLE

HOW TO PLAY:

 Knock Vampires down using the thunder5 or rifle then stake
the body using the pool cue to kill them completely. 
 Use the secondary attack with pool cue to knock vampires
away.
 Use secondary attack on rifle to zoom.
 To change team press M (default) or ENTER whilst dead.
